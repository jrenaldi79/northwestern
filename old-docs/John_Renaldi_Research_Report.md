#### Acquisition Details

**Acquirer:** Life360, Inc. (ASX: 360)
**Deal Value:** $37 million (upfront), potentially up to $54.5 million with earnout
**Deal Structure:** Primarily stock and debt; 765,733 shares (2,297,199 CDIs), convertible promissory notes ($11.5M face value), 68,328 employee options

**Integration:** All 25 Jiobit employees joined Life360; John Renaldi continued as part of the leadership team initially

**Source:** ASX announcements (April 27, 2021; September 2, 2021); TechCrunch

#### Product & Technology

**Product Description:** World's smallest and longest-lasting real-time location tracking platform

**Target Markets:**
- Young children
- Pets
- Seniors
- Government/B2B (later expansion)

**Technical Innovations:**
- Patented Progressive Beaconing technology
- Sensor fusion combining GPS, cellular, WiFi, and Bluetooth
- Custom system architecture
- Encryption and security technologies
- Waterproof, durable form factor

**Key Quote (John Renaldi, 2016):**
> "Long lasting battery life and simple integration into our clothing is just the beginning. This disruptive technology will affect products for kids, adults, and other tangential spaces in the near future."

**Key Quote (Inventors Digest, 2019):**
> "For any of this to work... we had to nail this custom system architecture and the sensor fusion technology."

**Market Validation:**
- Triple-digit growth rates (per Life360 acquisition announcement)
- Sold out of product during 2018 holiday season
- Usage in "every populated continent in the world"
- Trusted by federal government customers and law enforcement

### Motorola Mobility (Pre-2015)

**Roles:** Multiple product and technology leadership positions
**Key Achievement:** Created Moto Maker / Moto X—the first built-to-order direct-to-consumer smartphone business

#### Moto Maker Details

**Launch:** August 2013 (initially AT&T exclusive)
**Expansion:** November 2013 (Sprint, T-Mobile, Verizon)
**Manufacturing:** Fort Worth, Texas plant
**Shipping:** 4 days or less for custom orders
**Customization Options:** 2,000+ permutations

**Features John Led:**
- Customer-facing design customization interface
- Manufacturing integration for built-to-order production
- UX designed around "Paradox of Choice" research
- Partnership with Sol Republic for matching accessories

**John Renaldi Quote (Digital Trends Interview):**
> "There's a concept called the Ikea effect. The Harvard Business Review put it out. And the Ikea effect says that if you have a hand in creating something, your affinity for that product goes through the roof. It's this idea that labor creates love."

> "By curating these colors, we saw that anxiety go away, and we saw them actually click around more and try out different combinations."

**Recognition:**
- Webby Awards
- Best of CES
- Red Dot Design Award
- Featured by Guy Kawasaki
